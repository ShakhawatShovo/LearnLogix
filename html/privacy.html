Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2025

At LearnLogix, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Your name, email, and student ID during registration
  • Course progress and completion data
  • Certificate download history
  • Usage behavior (for improving the platform)

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your learning dashboard
  • Issue and track certificates
  • Improve platform performance
  • Communicate updates (if opted in)

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Your information is stored securely in our database and is never shared with third parties without consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Correct inaccurate information

Contact us at support@learnlogix.com for any privacy-related requests.
